The Clear Seas Standard

Owning a marine aquarium should be a source of tranquility, not a list of chores. At Clear Seas, we take the technical burden off your shoulders. Whether you have a complex SPS-dominant reef or a high-traffic commercial display, our professional aquarium maintenance services ensure your system remains a masterpiece.

"We don't just clean tanks; we manage delicate biological ecosystems using 25+ years of reef-keeping expertise."

Our Comprehensive Maintenance Checklist:

  • ✔️ Precision Water Changes: Utilizing our 1-micron filtered natural seawater from San Remo.
  • ✔️ Equipment Optimization: Deep cleaning and calibration of protein skimmers, wavemakers, and return pumps.
  • ✔️ Scientific Chemistry Analysis: On-site testing paired with Triton ICP lab results for absolute elemental precision.
  • ✔️ Algae & Substrate Management: Mechanical removal of nuisance algae and professional gravel vacuuming.
