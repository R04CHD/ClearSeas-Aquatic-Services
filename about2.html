The Foundation of a Thriving Reef

At Clear Seas Aquatic Services, we believe that the foundation of a thriving reef begins with the water. Our journey didn't start as a business; it started over 25 years ago with a deep-seated passion for marine biology and the complex art of reef-keeping.

"We recognised a critical gap: the need for high-purity natural seawater that avoids the heavy pollutants of Port Phillip Bay."

Having spent decades navigating the pet industry, our founders mastered the delicate chemistry of marine environments. We invested in state-of-the-art logistics, capable of transporting up to 4,000 liters of natural seawater directly to your door.

Why Experience Matters

In an industry where a slight shift in alkalinity or a trace metal spike can compromise years of coral growth, experience is your best insurance policy.

  • Decades of Insight: 25+ years of troubleshooting complex marine systems.
  • Scientific Rigor: Every batch is backed by our strict collection protocols.
  • Elemental Precision: We utilize Triton ICP testing for absolute quality guarantee.

Our Quality Promise

We source our water from San Remo at the peak of the incoming tide, ensuring the purest Bass Strait water reaches your tank. If you aren't satisfied, we offer a full refund.
